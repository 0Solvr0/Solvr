Solvr

Solve a real 3×3 Rubik's Cube — snap photos of it or color a 3D model. Then watch Solvr turn the cube for you, one move at a time, at whatever speed feels comfy.

1. Take a photo of each side

Every side of the cube has a middle sticker whose color never changes. The easiest way: just match each photo to its middle color below — tap a box and snap that side so it fills the picture.

Not sure how to hold the cube?
You don't have to! Each box is labeled by its middle color, and the middle never moves. Find the side with a white middle and photograph it for the “White / Top” box, the side with a green middle for “Green / Front,” and so on. Hold the cube straight so the side fills the frame.

2. Check & fix the colors

Cameras can misread a sticker under uneven light. Pick a color, then click any sticker in the map to fix it. The six middle stickers are locked.

Color your cube

Drag to spin it around. Pick a color below, then click stickers to paint them. The six middle stickers are locked to the standard scheme. When you solve, this same cube turns itself to show you every move.

3D view unavailable — use the flat map below.

Solve & watch

Color every sticker, then solve. Press play and the cube turns itself — slow it down if the moves go too fast.

How orientation works
The solution assumes you hold the cube with white Up, green Front, red Right, yellow Down, orange Left, blue Back — matching the fixed middle stickers.
Runs entirely in your browser · your photos never leave your device · the solver may take ~5s to warm up the first time.
