How to read the moves
🛠️

4×4 solver — in progress

1. Take a photo of each side

Every side of the cube has a middle sticker whose color never changes. The easiest way: just match each photo to its middle color below — tap a box and snap that side so it fills the picture. For the best read, use bright even light, hold the cube straight, and keep it steady (avoid blur).

Not sure how to hold the cube?
You don't have to! Each box is labeled by its middle color, and the middle never moves. Find the side with a white middle and photograph it for the “White / Top” box, the side with a green middle for “Green / Front,” and so on. Hold the cube straight so the side fills the frame.

2. Check & fix the colors

Cameras can misread a sticker under uneven light. Pick a color, then click any sticker in the map to fix it. The six middle stickers are locked.

Scan your puzzle

Take a straight-on photo of each face, framed to fill the square and turned the same way as the diagram. We'll read the colors as a starting point — then you can fix any misreads on the 3D model before solving.
